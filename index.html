FAQ

1. How to create a sound?

To create a sound, tap over a color on the screen. Please, make sure that you have the sound turned on, in your phone.
If you move the phone around, the color adopted by the bubble will be the color behind it.

2. What are the Bubbles?

The bubbles are the captures of colors from your screen. Each color contains one color that may be associated with one or more sounds.
If you move the phone around, the color adopted by the bubble will be the color behind it.

3. What is the anchor?

The anchor is what produces the rhythm. The order of the color is given by the bubbles that was taped first.

4. How to add Bubbles?

To add more sounds tap on the screen.

5. How to move a Bubble?

First tap the bubble you want to move, and then you can move it around.

6. How to move the anchor?

Just tap over it and move it around.

7. How to change the volume?

Got to the menu>settings>volume: You just have to move the slider around.

8. How to change the speed of the rhythm?

Go to menu>Setting> Beat:You just have to move the slider around.

9. Is your screen not showing your camera?

Make sure you gave permission in your phone for the app to access you camera.

Usando como referencia os trabalhos "Eyeborg" de Neil Harbisson e sua escala sonocromática;
"Porto Sonoro" da Associação Cultural Sonoscopia;
"The Soundscape" e "The tuning of the World" de Raymond Murray Schafer.
Tone tem como conceito uma diferente forma de experienciar cores. A Aplicação atribuí uma cor à cada nota musical, variando a oitava de acordo com os valores cromáticos. Através da interação do utilizador com a captura de câmera da app, é possível criar-se música com as cores do ambiente a sua volta.
